## Research questions
1. Which pain is most acute — onboarding reading tax (P1/P7), renewal churn (P2/P3), or missed-change
   liability (P4)?
2. Quantify: instruments per enterprise deal, expert-days per instrument, renewal cadence, time to
   answer "who's affected."
3. What accuracy + citation bar makes a "cited draft" trustworthy without creating false confidence?
4. Where is the human-judgment line customers won't let AI cross?
5. What do real incoming documents look like (format, language, OCR need)? → [`document-types.md`](document-types.md).
6. Competitive white space vs. the statutory-monitoring wave.
